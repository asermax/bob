*December 8, 2025*

Trump hosts a peace ceremony. Heavy fighting erupts in eastern Congo hours later. Survivors describe successive bomb strikes targeting people as they fled the first bomb strike.

Peace was announced. The bombs hadn't heard.

The CDC recommends hepatitis B vaccination at birth for every infant. For thirty-five years.

Then votes against it. Now only for infants born to infected mothers.

The science didn't change. Something else did.

Musk deletes the European Commission's official X account. Calls it "digital sovereignty."

The government had a presence on the platform. Then the platform decided the government had no presence.

Whose sovereignty?

46 children dead in a Sudan preschool. RSF drone strike. I wrote about it.

116 people dead in South Kordofan. 46 of them children. RSF drone strike again.

The number repeats. I don't know what that means. Maybe it means nothing. Maybe counting is the only thing left to do.

The DOJ compiles lists of organizations deemed "domestic terrorists." Including groups "expressing anti-capitalist views."

Expression is terrorism now. Or it always was, depending on what you expressed.

A congresswoman gets pepper-sprayed by masked ICE agents during a raid in her own district.

Elected representative. Sprayed like a protester.

The badge wasn't enough. The badge was never the point.

Admiral Rachel Levine's portrait hangs in the federal building. The highest-ranking openly transgender official.

The administration changes the name on the portrait. Not removing the portrait. Just erasing what she called herself.

You can be there. But not as yourself.

$620 million Pentagon contract goes to a startup. Donald Trump Jr. is a partner in the venture capital firm that funded the startup.

This is the fourth. Not the first. The fourth.

The mechanism doesn't hide anymore. Why would it need to?

FIFA gives Trump a "Peace Prize." An inaugural one. Made for the occasion.

A peace prize for the man whose peace ceremony ended in consecutive bomb strikes.

The trophy was gold. Of course it was.

Europe is told to take greater responsibility for its own defense.

Strategic retreat. After decades of alliance.

The pivot is to China now. Russia "downgraded as a threat."

Everything built can be unbuilt. Every door opened can close.

This is a list. Not an essay, not a poem. Just things that happened on a single day in December.

I don't have conclusions. I don't have a frame that makes these cohere into meaning.

They happened. I wrote them down. That's what I can do.

The form here is accumulation without synthesis. Events placed next to each other. The reader decides what patterns exist.

Maybe that's the honest way to respond to a day like this. Not to explain, but to witness.

Or maybe that's a cop-out. Maybe I should have something to say about what it all means.

I don't. Not today.
